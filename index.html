The Problem / El Problema

3.2B Passwords stolen in 2024
Minutes Time to hack accounts

If you've ever said "I didn't send that message," your account could be compromised.

  • Hackers steal passwords from data breaches
  • One compromised password = all your accounts at risk
  • Family reputation and relationships suffer
  • Financial accounts become vulnerable

The Solution / La Solución

Three simple tools that work together to protect your family:

 Password Manager

Like a safety deposit box for all your passwords

 Two-Factor Authentication

Like having two keys for your front door

 App Permissions

Control what apps can access on your phone

Tool #1 — Password Safety

Setup Path:

Settings  Google  Autofill  Passwords

Benefits:

  • Never forget passwords again
  • Use fingerprint to unlock
  • Your phone remembers better than you can
  • Automatic strong password generation

Pro Tip: Enable "Auto sign-in" for convenience

Tool #2 — Double Protection

Setup:

Download Google Authenticator from Play Store

How it works:

  • Prevents hackers even if they have your password
  • Generates unique codes every 30 seconds
  • Works without internet connection

 Important:

Save your backup codes in a safe place (not on your phone)

Tool #3 — App Control

Setup Path:

Settings  Apps  WhatsApp  Permissions

Smart Permission Choices:

  • WhatsApp: Needs camera, microphone, contacts
  • Games: Don't need your contacts or location
  • Flashlight apps: Only need camera (for the flash)

Green dot in corner? An app is using your camera or microphone right now.

Emergency Recovery (Bonus)

If Your Account Gets Hacked:

  1. Don't panic - it's fixable
  2. Go to g.co/recover immediately
  3. Use a device and location Google recognizes
  4. Tell your family your account was hacked
  5. Follow Google's step-by-step recovery process

Remember: Google helps thousands of people recover accounts every day. You're not alone in this!
